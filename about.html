The Story of Blue Orchids

Founded in Magarpatta City, Blue Orchids The Beauty Hub was built with one purpose — to create a safe, professional and empowering beauty space exclusively for women.

With over 9 years of excellence, the salon has grown into one of the most trusted beauty destinations in Hadapsar and Magarpatta.

Every service reflects our commitment to hygiene, quality products and expert care. We proudly use premium brands like L’Oreal, O3+ and Rica.

Vani Verma

Founder & Owner
