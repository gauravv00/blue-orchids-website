Our Story

Blue Orchids The Beauty Hub was founded in Magarpatta City with a vision to create a safe, hygienic and welcoming beauty space exclusively for women.

Over the last 9+ years, the salon has grown into one of the most trusted beauty destinations in Hadapsar and Magarpatta. Every service focuses on professional care, hygiene and premium products.

Blue Orchids proudly uses internationally trusted brands such as L’Oreal, O3+ and Rica, ensuring that every treatment delivers visible results while maintaining the highest safety standards.

Today the salon continues to empower women by providing a space where clients feel confident, comfortable and cared for.

Vani Verma

Founder & Owner

A Space Designed For Women

Blue Orchids is more than just a salon. It is a comfortable sanctuary where women can relax and enjoy professional beauty services in a clean, accessible and friendly environment. The salon is designed to maintain high hygiene standards while providing premium beauty treatments that help clients feel confident and refreshed.
