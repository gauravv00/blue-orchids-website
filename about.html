The Story of Blue Orchids

Home / Our Story

Founded with Purpose

Built for Women,
by a Woman

Blue Orchids was founded in 2017 by Vani Verma with one clear purpose — to create a safe, professional, and empowering beauty space exclusively for the women of Magarpatta City.

Trained professionally in cosmetology and beauty therapy, Vani built what began as an intimate studio into one of Hadapsar's most trusted beauty destinations — serving thousands of women over nine years.

"Every woman who walks through our doors deserves to be treated like royalty. That belief hasn't changed since day one."

— Vani Verma, Founder & Owner
